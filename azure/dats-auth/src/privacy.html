DATS Accessible Booking Assistant

TL;DR: We temporarily store an encrypted session cookie (not your password) in Azure Canada for 24 hours. You can delete it anytime with the disconnect_account command.

What Data We Collect

When Using Remote Mode (Claude Mobile/Web)

When you connect your DATS account through Claude mobile or web, we store:

  • Encrypted DATS session cookie - Allows us to make API calls to DATS on your behalf
  • DATS client ID - Your DATS account identifier (e.g., "12345")
  • Session timestamp - When your session was created
‚úÖ What we DON'T collect:
  • Your DATS username or passcode (entered directly in your browser, never sent to us)
  • Trip details (addresses, times, destinations - fetched in real-time from DATS)
  • Personal health information
  • Names, phone numbers, or other identifying information
  • Browsing history or device information

When Using Local Mode (Claude Desktop)

When using Claude Desktop, all session data stays on your computer. Nothing is sent to our servers.

How We Store Your Data

Encryption

  • Algorithm: AES-256-GCM (industry standard, same as banks)
  • At rest: Session cookies encrypted before storage in Cosmos DB
  • In transit: All connections use TLS 1.2+

Data Residency

  • Location: Azure Canada Central (Toronto region)
  • Compliance: Protection of Privacy Act (Alberta)
  • Cross-border: Data never leaves Canada

Automatic Deletion

  • Time-to-Live (TTL): 24 hours
  • Mechanism: Cosmos DB automatic cleanup
  • No backups: Once deleted, sessions are permanently gone

Why We Store This Data

Session cookies enable:

  1. Booking trips through Claude mobile/web without re-entering credentials
  2. Viewing your trips across multiple conversations
  3. Real-time tracking of your DATS vehicles

Legal basis: Your explicit consent (POPA Section 7)

Your Rights Under POPA

1. Right to Access

You can request a copy of the data we store about you. Email: privacy@dats-booking.ca

2. Right to Delete

Delete your session anytime:

  • Immediately: Use disconnect_account command
  • Automatically: Wait 24 hours for TTL expiration

3. Right to Withdraw Consent

Disconnect your account to withdraw consent. You'll need to re-consent if you want to use the service again.

4. Right to Correct

Session data is temporary and read-only. For corrections to your DATS account info, contact DATS directly.

Audit Logging

We log security-relevant events for accountability:

  • Consent given/withdrawn
  • Session created/deleted
  • Authentication attempts

Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach:

  1. We will notify affected users within 72 hours (POPA requirement)
  2. We will notify the Alberta Privacy Commissioner
  3. We will provide details of what was compromised and mitigation steps

Contact for breach reports: security@dats-booking.ca

Children's Privacy

This service is designed for adults who are eligible for DATS services. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18.
